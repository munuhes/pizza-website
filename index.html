About Us

Naruto is a family business located in Nairobi, Kenya. We offer traditional Neapolitan style pizza made from nothing but fresh and local ingredients! Our recipes have been tried and tested for three generations. All our pizzas are cooked in our custom made wood fired oven which was designed and manufactured in Italy.

Feel free to visit us at Ecobank Towers Starehe, Kaunda street, Nairobi to have a taste of our pizzas! Can't make it? Feel free to place an order online.
